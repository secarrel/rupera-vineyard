Things to Enjoy

Walk through the vines

Our 25-acre vineyard is a beautiful place for a walk. We allow dogs if they’re kept on leads, and we offer guided tours if you’d like to learn more about what you see!

Taste our wines

We have a number of wines available for tasting. You can try a good selection of them on a wine tasting or order a glass in our cafe!

A beautiful environment to work

We have special quiet spaces for you to tuck away and type away! We know that sometimes you just need a change of scenery, so we welcome you to make our café your office for the day!

Join us for lunch

We have a fantastic supply of local produce which our talented chef puts together to create nutritious, healthy and hearty dishes which we are sure you will love.

Relax with a coffee

We serve fantastic coffee in whatever form you like. Our supplier in Cardiff is an independent and small company who recently started roasting. We support local!

Explore our magical meadows

Butterflies, bees, grasshoppers and birds! If you love a walk and you love nature, you’ll find yourself in heaven in our meadows. We try to encourage wildlife by leaving our meadows wild, but we have set aside a field for a fantastic sunflower display as well.

Enjoy our evening events

From one off celebrations to regular meets, we love putting on events for our visitors. Our events are always popular, so we recommend getting in touch to save a spot!

There are plenty of reasons to visit us at Rupera Vineyard.
Whether you fancy a scenic stroll through our vines and meadows, a delicious lunch that hits the spot (in a beautiful spot), or a tour and taste of our wines to learn about the fastest growing agricultural industry in the UK, we'll make sure you get everything you hoped from your visit, and more.

If you'd like to book a table, a tour or one of our events, please get in touch!

Weekly Events


Live Band and pizza

Enjoy hearing talented musicians from the local area? Then friday nights are the time to visit. If that isn't enough, our stone pizza oven will be hot and ready to make you a dreamy pizza of your craetion. Thirsty? Our cafe turns into a cosy bar at night to supply you with your beverage of choice.


Taco Tuesdays

Our mexican themed evenings are held every tuesday and are always popular. We have a local mariachi band that plays for us every week while you get the chance to induldge on tacos and nachos. The cafe transforms to a Margharita and Mexican Mule making machine, so you always have a delicious mexican cocktail to wash down the tacos.


Cheese and Wine Evening

Celebrate local wines and those from a little further afield with the perfect accompaniment... cheese! We have a fantastic selection of cheese, some local, which are specially chosen to pair with the evening's wines. A relaxing and intimate evening to share with other wine and cheese enthusiasts.
